In-hand salary, HRA aur income tax samajhna itna mushkil kyun hai?

Jab offer letter mein CTC likha hota hai, wo pura number kabhi bank account mein nahi aata. Usme PF, gratuity, aur kai baar insurance jaisi cheezein already ghusi hoti hain jo aapko directly nahi milti. Yahi confusion sabse zyada freshers aur job-switchers ko hoti hai — is page ke teeno calculators isi gap ko fill karne ke liye bane hain.

1. CTC aur In-Hand salary mein fark

CTC (Cost to Company) mein Basic, HRA, Special Allowance ke saath-saath Employer ka PF contribution aur Gratuity bhi jud jaata hai — ye dono paisa aapko turant nahi milta. Isliye in-hand salary hamesha CTC se kam hoti hai, generally CTC ka 70-80% ke aas-paas.

2. HRA exemption ka fayda kaise uthayein

Agar aap rent pe rehte ho aur Old Tax Regime choose karte ho, to HRA ka ek hissa completely tax-free ho sakta hai. Exemption teen values mein se sabse chhoti value hoti hai — actual HRA, rent minus 10% basic, ya 50%/40% of basic. Landlord ka PAN dena zaroori hota hai agar annual rent ₹1 lakh se zyada hai.

3. Old vs New Tax Regime — kaun better hai?

FY 2025-26 se New Regime default hai aur ₹12 lakh tak taxable income par effectively zero tax lagta hai (87A rebate ki wajah se). Lekin agar aapke paas bade deductions hain — jaise home loan interest, 80C investments, ya high HRA — to Old Regime kabhi kabhi zyada fayda de sakta hai. Isi liye dono compare karna zaroori hai, sirf ek regime maan kar mat chalo.

Kya ye calculator official Income Tax calculator ki jagah le sakta hai?

Nahi. Ye ek quick estimate tool hai jo common assumptions (surcharge, marginal relief jaise complex cases chhod kar) use karta hai. Final ITR filing se pehle apne CA ya Income Tax Department ke official calculator se verify zaroor karein.

PF aur gratuity ka % har company mein same hota hai?

Employee PF generally 12% of Basic fixed hai, lekin kuch companies Basic ki definition alag rakhti hain, aur small companies mein PF apply hi nahi hota (agar employee count kam hai). Apni exact salary structure HR se confirm karein.

4. SIP se long-term corpus kaise banta hai

SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) mein har mahine chhoti amount invest karke, saalon mein compounding ka fayda milta hai. Jitna zyada time market mein rehta hai, utna zyada returns "returns par returns" ki wajah se badhte hain — isiliye jaldi shuru karna, amount se zyada matter karta hai.

5. EMI lene se pehle kya dekhna chahiye

Loan lene se pehle total interest payable zaroor check karein — kam tenure mein EMI zyada lagti hai but total interest kam jaata hai. Rule of thumb: total EMI (sab loans milakar) aapki in-hand salary ke 40% se zyada nahi honi chahiye.

Professional tax kya hai?

Ye state government ka tax hai jo kuch states mein lagta hai (Maharashtra, Karnataka, West Bengal, etc.) — amount state-wise alag hota hai, generally ₹200/month ke aas-paas. Kuch states (jaise Delhi, Haryana, UP) mein professional tax hai hi nahi.
